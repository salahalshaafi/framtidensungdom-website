About Us

Who are we?

We are a group of dedicated individuals who believe in the potential of young people to contribute positively to society. Future Youth was founded in 2024 with the aim of protecting young people from risks such as substance abuse and crime, as well as creating a safe environment for personal development.

We work to build bridges between youth and society through mentorship and support programs. Our vision is that every young person should have the opportunity to shape their future in a positive way.

Our goals

  • Reduce drug use and crime among youth by 20% within the next three years.
  • Create a safe and supportive environment for youth through psychological and educational support.
  • Promote integration and community through cultural and sports activities.
  • Inspire young people with positive role models and replace destructive influences with successful individuals in sports, work life, and society.
  • Digital enlightenment and awareness spreading through social media to reach more young people with important messages.

The association's goals

  • Launch a 24/7 hotline for parents who are worried about their children and want to know how to act appropriately. The hotline is also for young people who are caught up in crime or addiction and don't know how to get out of it.
  • Offer concrete and realistic solutions with complete confidentiality to protect everyone seeking help and support.

Made By youth to youth

The Future's Youth is a non-profit organization that works to support young people in Sweden, especially those with a migration background, and help them avoid drugs, crime, and social exclusion. Through education, sports activities, and psychosocial support, we empower youth and guide them towards a safer and more successful future.
